Location

The conference will be held in the University Park campus of the University of Nottingham.

Travel

The best way to get to Nottingham is by train. In order to get to the conference venue from Nottingham station, the fastest way is to take the tram (Toton Lane direction). Alternatively, there is a 50 minutes walk from the station to the conference venue. 

Accommodation

We expect to be able to provide accommodation for most speakers.  The list of speakers will be confirmed after registration closes.
