Location

The conference will be held in the Fry Building School of Mathematics at the University of Bristol.

Travel

The best way to get to Bristol is by train. In order to get to the conference venue from Bristol Temple Meads train station, you can either walk or take the local bus number 8. Both methods of transport take about 30 minutes long.

Accommodation

We expect to be able to provide accommodation for all speakers.  The list of speakers will be confirmed after registration closes.
