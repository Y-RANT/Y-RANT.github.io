Young Researchers in Algebraic Number Theory (Y-RANT) 


Y-RANT is an annual conference primarily aimed at early career researchers in the UK (Ph.D. students and postdocs) working in or interested in algebraic number theory, analytic number theory and related topics. This is the sixth edition of the conference, which will be hosted at the University of Oxford.

This is a great chance to meet other early career number theorists, get a feeling for the interests of the broader community, and discuss your ideas in a friendly and engaging environment.

All participants are strongly encouraged to give a talk, either on their own research, or on a topic of interest. In addition, there will be plenary talks given by Sir Andrew Wiles and  Hanneke Wiersema.
