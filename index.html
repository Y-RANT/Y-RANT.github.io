Young Researchers in Algebraic Number Theory (Y-RANT)

Y-RANT is an annual conference aimed at early career researchers (Ph.D. students and postdocs) working in or interested in algebraic number theory, analytic number theory and related topics. This is the fourth edition of the conference, which will be hosted at the University of Glasgow.

This is a great chance to meet other early career number theorists, get a feeling for the interests of the broader community, and discuss your ideas in a friendly and engaging environment.

All participants are strongly encouraged to give a talk, either on their own research, or on a topic of interest. In addition, there will be three keynote talks given by senior researchers in the field.
