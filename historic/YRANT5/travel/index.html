Location

The conference will be held in the Centre for Mathematical Sciences (CMS) and the University of Cambridge.

Travel

The CMS is about a 45 minute walk away from Cambridge Railway Station.  If you wish to use public transport then we recommend taking the U bus to Wilberforce Road.

Accommodation

We expect to be able to provide accommodation for most speakers.  The list of speakers will be confirmed after registration closes.
