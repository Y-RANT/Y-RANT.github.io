Young Researchers in Algebraic Number Theory (Y-RANT) 

Y-RANT is an annual conference primarily aimed at early career researchers in the UK (Ph.D. students and postdocs) working in or interested in algebraic number theory, analytic number theory and related topics. This is the fourth edition of the conference, which will be hosted at the University of Glasgow.

This is a great chance to meet other early career number theorists, get a feeling for the interests of the broader community, and discuss your ideas in a friendly and engaging environment.

All participants are strongly encouraged to give a talk, either on their own research, or on a topic of interest. In addition, there will be three keynote talks given by Jessica Fintzen, James Newton, Rachel Newton.

Conference Details

Dates: Tuesday 23rd August -- Thursday 25th August 2022
Location: University of Glasgow
Registration Deadline: 29th May 2022 (see registration page)
