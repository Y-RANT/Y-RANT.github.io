Travel

The ARC is very close to Kelvinhall subway station.  If you wish to travel by train then the shortest routes are:

  • Train to Glasgow Queen Street -- Subway from Buchannan St. to Kelvinhall
  • Train to Glasgow Central -- Subway from St. Enoch Centre to Kelvinhall

The subway is one circuit, and so there is no "wrong line" for getting here; however the inner line will be the shorter route from the two train stations to Kelvinhall.
